# Why I only take three clients a month - and why this matters for yours

Have you ever worked with a coach who seemed burned out?

I take coaching very seriously. Your transformation is my top priority, and that means I don't overload my schedule. After 20 years of teaching and coaching - sometimes seeing up to eight people in a day - I know exactly what happens when a coach is stretched too thin.

## What happens to a coach who's taken on too many clients

A stretched coach runs on empty. That changes the quality of everything a client pays for.

Three things slip, every time:

- They run on empty - presence flattens and energy drops inside the session
- They confuse clients or lose track of progress from one session to the next
- They can't give you 100% attention in the room, which is precisely what you're paying for

I've also been the client of brilliant coaches who simply didn't have anything left in the tank. When you're paying VIP rates, that's a waste of money.

## What a properly bespoke coaching experience looks like

That's why I founded Authentic Communicator Global. I wanted to build a premium coaching experience that is genuinely bespoke, not bespoke-branded.

My private clients get:

- Unlimited WhatsApp support between sessions
- Written or voice feedback on recordings they send me between sessions
- Event attendance when the stakes justify it
- Flexible hours around their actual schedule, not a 9-to-5 grid
- A standing cap on my own caseload so I can keep all of the above true

You might expect this level of dedication to be very expensive. But because I keep the business lean, my services are often more affordable than the large training companies that subcontract the actual coaching out.

## Questions to ask any coach before you hire them

If you're considering coaching, ask three questions of whoever you're thinking of working with:

- Is this coach burned out?
- How many clients are they juggling right now?
- Will they actually be there for me, inside and outside the session?

If you can't get a clear, confident answer to all three, keep looking. Investing in your growth deserves a coach who has the energy, focus, and commitment to match.
